The Closers Club

Two teens with supernatural powers and a school haunted by ghosts

Upon starting in an intensive therapeutic boarding school, Mirabelle Santiago, a sensitive empath with the ability to absorb other's feelings and emotions, and Tristan Bunker, a troubled teen with the rare ability to see ghosts, soon realize that their new school is riddled with ghosts.

As the two teens reluctantly join forces to solve the mystery behind the dark entity that forced them to crash together, not only do they uncover dark secrets about the school's ghostly inhabitants, but even darker secrets lurking under the school's brightly painted walls.

Dark secrets that can prove to be deadlier than the evil spirit that haunts them...

The Obsidian Prophecy

The fate of the world lies in the hands of the brave few that dare to defy destiny.

The prophecy foretold of war and hate, of the known world torn apart by the imbalance of light and darkness. Only four beings, empowered with the ability to wield the elements, would be able to restore balance to the world once again.

It would seem that fate would dictate the lives of Faeolyn, Evelyn, Nadyanelle, and Az'ria, the four chosen Elements. But our heroines do not abide by the laws of destiny. And neither do the heroes that join their side.

Will the delicate balance of good and evil, of light and darkness, withstand the opposition of that which was fated to be?

The Dragon's Prophecy

As of April 2018, The Dragon'Prophecy has been discontinued

After careful deliberation and research, I've made the executive decision to rewrite my first novel, The Dragon's Prophecy. The flaws in this first installment are too many and too great to continue the trilogy as originally planned.

Equipped with more knowledge and storytelling sense, I am confident I can write a better story with a compelling plot and dynamic characters.

As we all wait in breathless anticipation (this sounds terribly familiar...), you can look forward to character designs, worldbuilding designs, and bonus stories.
